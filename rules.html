Rules

The goal of the game is to survive the Maze of the Minotaur. You will spend ten rounds trying to fend off mobs, while gathering coins and food. After those ten rounds you will face off against the minotaur. If you defeat him, the game will end. But failure will result in losing a life and having to go through more of the dungeon befoire facing off again. Througout the dungeon you will collect coins, and your highest coin collection in one run will be displayed.

When you finish one room, you are able to go in one of three directions, left, right, and forward. To go left, press A. To go straight, press W. To go left press D. Choose wisely, because choosing the wrong one result in death.

You start the game with three health and five food. If either of these values goes to zero, you lose. You will lose one food every round and in many rooms there will be food thast you can use to keep your character alive. You lose one health every time you lose to a monster and it cannot be regained.

when facing a monster, you will engage in combat. There are three moves that your character can do. These are punch (press P), kick (press K), and block (press b). Each monster has a set move that it can use. Check the bestiary for clues on this topic.
